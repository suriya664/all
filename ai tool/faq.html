General Questions

What is AI TOOL?
AI TOOL is a comprehensive AI platform that provides businesses with advanced artificial intelligence tools and services. We offer AI chatbots, analytics, security solutions, and cloud infrastructure to help organizations transform their operations.
How do I get started?
Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for a free trial account on our registration page. You'll have access to all features during the 14-day trial period. No credit card required.
What programming languages do you support?
Our API supports all major programming languages including Python, JavaScript, Java, Ruby, PHP, Go, and more. We provide comprehensive SDKs and documentation for each language.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-leading security measures including end-to-end encryption, SOC 2 compliance, regular security audits, and data encryption at rest and in transit. Your data is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.
Can I integrate AI TOOL with my existing systems?
Yes! AI TOOL offers extensive integration capabilities through our REST API and webhooks. We integrate with popular platforms like Salesforce, Slack, Zapier, and many others. Custom integrations are available for Enterprise plans.
What kind of support do you offer?
Support varies by plan. Starter plans include email support with 48-hour response time. Pro plans include priority email support with 24-hour response time. Enterprise plans include 24/7 dedicated support via phone, email, and chat, plus a dedicated account manager.
Do you offer training or onboarding?
Yes! All plans include access to our documentation, tutorials, and video guides. Pro plans include live onboarding sessions. Enterprise plans include comprehensive training programs and ongoing support from our experts.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied.
What happens if I exceed my API call limit?
If you exceed your monthly API call limit, we'll notify you immediately. You can either upgrade your plan for higher limits or purchase additional API calls as needed. We never cut off service without warning.
Do you offer custom AI model development?
Yes, custom AI model development is available for Enterprise customers. Our team works with you to understand your specific needs and develops tailored AI solutions. Contact our sales team to discuss your requirements.
